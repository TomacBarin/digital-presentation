Why Fullstack?

Why Chas?

[ Two threads pull me here. ]

Professional.

I'm drawn to small, passionate companies that sell unique products. Think obscure films, indie games, arcade pinball parts - or even retro fashion, like the HepCat store, which specializes in distinctive vintage apparel and rare vinyl albums. These businesses often run lean, relying on versatile teams, and I thrive in that collaborative energy.
Through the Chas Academy Fullstack Developer Program, I'm gaining the skills to build and maintain responsive, user-friendly websites with robust backend systems. My goal is simple: to be the go-to developer for a small company with a quirky, niche product, helping them share their passion with the world through a website that truly shines.

Personal.

I am one skill away from creating my first shmup. With a background in 3D art and sound design, programming is the final piece I need to bring my vision to life. The Chas Academy Fullstack Developer Program allows me to master JavaScript, which I'll combine with Phaser for my initial prototypes, before advancing to C# and Unity to craft full-blown bullet hell shmups. Drawing inspiration from the dynamic intensity of Crimzon Clover and the creative flair of Angel at Dusk, both created by solo developers, I'm excited to code my own games.
